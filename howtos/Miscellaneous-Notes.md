## Gutenberg ZIM torrent

Kiwix publishes a ZIM of the full English Project Gutenberg library (books + covers, ~70k titles). Faster to grab via torrent than rsync if you just want the ZIM for Kiwix rather than the raw EPUBs.

Torrent: https://download.kiwix.org/zim/gutenberg/gutenberg_en_all.zim.torrent

If you want the raw EPUBs for Calibre instead, see [mirror-gutenberg-epubs.md](mirror-gutenberg-epubs.md).
